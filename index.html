Welcome to FinTrack

Your personal finance tracker — works on all your devices, keeps your data private and in sync.

⚠️
First time here? The app loads with sample data so you can explore all features. When you're ready to track your real finances, go to Settings & Data → Reset All Data to clear it and start fresh.
✅

Quick Start — 4 Steps

1
Clear sample data

Go to Settings & Data → scroll to the bottom → click Reset All Data. This wipes the demo transactions so you start clean.

2
Set your financial month start day

Go to Settings & Data → Financial Month Start Day. If your salary arrives on the 22nd, set it to 22. This defines your monthly cycle for all reports.

3
Add your income

Click + Income in the top-right or go to the Income page. Enter your salary, freelance payments, or any earnings.

4
Log your expenses daily

Click + Expense (top-right) to quickly add a spend. Do this regularly for accurate reports.

📊

Dashboard

  • Shows 8 metric cards: total income, expenses, balance, savings rate, and the same 4 for the current financial month.
  • Click any month chip at the top to view a different month's summary.
  • The pie chart shows spending by category for the selected month.
  • The trend chart shows income vs. expense over the last 6 months.
  • The budget widget shows how much of each budget you've used this month.
💰

Income

  • Add all money you receive: salary, freelance, business, investments, gifts, refunds.
  • Each record needs: Amount, Date, Source (who paid you), and Category.
  • Use the search bar to find a specific income entry.
  • Filter by category or pick Specific Month from the date filter to see a single financial month.
  • Click column headers to sort the table.
💸

Expenses

  • Log every spend here: food, rent, EMI, fuel, shopping, subscriptions, etc.
  • Each record needs: Amount, Date, Category, and a short Description.
  • Time is optional but useful for daily tracking.
  • Filter by category, date range, or a specific financial month.
  • Click Edit on any row to update it, or Delete to remove it.
🔄

Transactions

  • A combined view of all income and expenses in one table.
  • Filter by type (income / expense), category, date range, or amount range.
  • The summary bar at the top shows totals for your current filter.
  • Export the filtered view to CSV, Excel, or PDF using the buttons on the right.
📈

Analytics

  • Spending Breakdown — doughnut chart showing expense share by category.
  • Income vs. Expense Trend — 6-month line chart showing income, expenses, and balance.
  • Top Categories — horizontal bar chart ranked by total spend.
  • Budget vs. Actual — grouped bar chart comparing your budget to real spend per category.
  • Use the date filter at the top to change the period for all charts.
📋

Reports

  • Financial Month Report — month-by-month summary for the last 6 FM cycles: income, expenses, savings, savings %, top category.
  • Yearly Report — full annual breakdown with monthly totals and annual averages.
  • Category Report — all-time totals per category, average transaction size, and % of total spend.
  • Export any report to PDF or Excel using the export buttons.
🎯

Budget

  • Set a Monthly Budget (overall spending limit for the FM) at the top.
  • Set category budgets — e.g. Food ₹8,000, Shopping ₹5,000.
  • Progress bars turn yellow at 75% and red at 100%.
  • Budget alerts also appear in the Dashboard budget widget.
  • Click Remove next to any category budget to delete it.
⚙️

Settings & Data

  • Financial Month Start Day — set the day your salary arrives (e.g. 22 = Jun 22 → Jul 21).
  • Currency — switch between ₹ INR, $ USD, € EUR, £ GBP.
  • Theme — toggle dark / light mode (also available in the sidebar).
  • Export your data as JSON, CSV, Excel, or PDF anytime.
  • Restore from Backup — import a previously exported JSON file to reload your data.
  • Reset All Data — permanently deletes everything from the cloud. Use this to clear the sample data when you start.
📱

Multi-Device Sync

  • Your data is stored in the cloud (Supabase), not just this browser.
  • Open the app on your phone or another laptop, sign in with the same email and password, and all your data is there.
  • Each user's data is completely private — no one else can see your records.
  • Sign out using the ⏻ button next to your email in the top-right.
📅

What is a Financial Month?

  • A Financial Month (FM) is a custom cycle that matches when you actually get paid, not the calendar month.
  • Example: if your start day is 22, then June FM = Jun 22 → Jul 21.
  • All Dashboard cards, reports, and budgets use your FM cycle.
  • You can change the start day anytime in Settings and everything recalculates instantly.
💡

Tips & Shortcuts

  • Use + Expense and + Income buttons in the header for quick entry from any page.
  • Click any column header in a table to sort by that column. Click again to reverse.
  • The sidebar can be collapsed (click ☰) to give you more screen space.
  • Back up your data regularly using Export JSON in Settings so you have a local copy.
  • Use Specific Month in the date filter on Income/Expense/Transaction pages to view any past FM.
